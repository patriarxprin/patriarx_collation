# PatriarX Collation
Within the framework of the PRIN2022 project PatrirX, the Research Units of Messina (Prof. Giuseppe Ucciarcello, postdoctoral researcher Federica Scognamiglio) and Basilicata (Prof. Elena Esposito, postdoctoral researcher Carmelo Nicolò Benvenuto) carried out the collations of the manuscript witnesses of the Lexicon of Cyril, identified on the basis of catalogues of Italian and foreign libraries and of the recensio codicum by Mark Naoumides, brought to light by the Research Unit of Verona (Prof. Paolo Scattolin, postdoctoral researcher Paola Carmela La Barbera).
The Messina unit carried out the collation of the manuscripts preserved in Italian libraries and in the Vatican City.
The Basilicata unit carried out the collation of the manuscripts preserved in foreign libraries.
